## 1. Introduction
**Kampung Web3** is a decentralized social governance experiment designed to digitize local community administration (RT/RW level) using blockchain technology.

## 2. Technical Architecture (V1 - V10)
The development was executed in 10 strategic phases to ensure system stability and modularity:
* **Governance**: Implementing on-chain hierarchical roles (RT to Camat).
* **Real Estate**: On-chain Land Certification using NFT-like resources.
* **Public Finance**: A transparent "Kas Desa" (Village Treasury) for public funding.
* **Social & Economy**: Siskamling (reporting), Marketplace, and a DAO-based "Gedung Serbaguna".
