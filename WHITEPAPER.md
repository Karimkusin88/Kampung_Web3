## 1. Introduction
**Kampung Web3** is a decentralized social governance experiment designed to digitize local community administration (RT/RW level).

## 2. Technical Architecture (V1 - V10)
The development was executed in 10 strategic phases:
* **Governance**: On-chain hierarchical roles (RT to Camat).
* **Real Estate**: Land Certification using NFT-like resources.
* **Public Finance**: Transparent "Kas Desa" (Village Treasury).
* **Social & Economy**: Siskamling, Marketplace, and DAO-based Community Center.
